## About Cafeteria Churrería San Pedro

**Cafeteria Churrería San Pedro** is located in *San Pedro, Tenerife (Canary Islands, Spain)*.  
We serve delicious **café con leche**, cold coffees, fresh sandwiches, and our famous **churros**, which are available **only on Fridays and Saturdays**.  
Our mission is to offer a warm, friendly environment with great food, great coffee, and fast service.

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works beautifully on all devices — mobile, tablet, and desktop.
- **Multi-language Support**: Available in Spanish, English, and German.
- **Menu Overview**: Clear presentation of our coffees, snacks, sandwiches, and churros.
- **Churros Availability**: Special notice that churros are served **Fridays & Saturdays only**.
- **Location Map**: Embedded Google Maps to help visitors find us easily.
- **Contact Options**: Easy redirection to our Facebook page and contact details.
